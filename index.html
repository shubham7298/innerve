What is INNERVE 2k18 ?

Innerve 2k18 Hackathon provides you the platform to showcase your ideas and technical skills at a recognized level. This is the perfect opportunity to get those brilliant ideas out of your amazing minds and onto the computer screens. Coming again this year Innerve brings you with more interesting and challenging problem statement than ever before.
